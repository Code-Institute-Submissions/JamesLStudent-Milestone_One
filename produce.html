INTERESTED IN WHERE WE SOURCE OUR INGREDIENTS?


All of our vegetables are sourced within 50 miles of the pub, and all of our meat products are sourced within 20 miles whenever possible. We try to source everything ethically, and whenever possible, our ingredients are kosher. If you're uncertain about anything, feel free to ask in-house, and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
