WELCOME TO THE OLD INN'S WEBSITE

Welcome to The Old Inn!
We all know that there are a lot of different types of pub - there are the football pubs, filled with people wearing the same colour shirt, and dives, filled with students who are only there because the beer is cheap and they want a good meeting place to get drunk. Then, there are the family-run pubs who serve good, wholesome food and provide a family atmosphere - and a children's play area for when the "family" part of the atmosphere gets impatient.

We're that last option - We hope to see you soon!
